Frequently Asked Questions

Clear any common misunderstanding and doubts about the application here.

  • How may I update the stock of the vaccine from the supply?

    You may scan the QR codes on the box and the shelves to automatically dynamically update the stock.

  • How does the automated QR code system work?

    The QR system utilizes scripts in order to assign each QR code with certain information regarding the doses of vaccines. This information can be communicated to the medical practitioners via an automatically updated database.

  • How does the information reach the medical practitioners?

    The QR codes help to update a dynamically changing database, which the medical professionals may get access to, thereby giving information about the stock of vaccines.
